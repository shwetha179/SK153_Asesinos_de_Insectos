Bundh Neerikshan is a specialized project for the monitoring of river embankments, by observing their hydro-metrological data in a regular basis or at times of crisis for forecasting the appropriate precautions to be taken. A health card is generated for the embankments and regular updates are issued by and to the local administration | project authorities | State Governments and other Central Ministries, based on which, alerts about the forecast are sent to the respective Government Staffs.
